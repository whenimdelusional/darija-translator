Privacy Policy

Darija Translator Chrome Extension  ·  Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Darija Translator ("we", "our", or "the extension") collects, uses, and protects your information. By using this extension, you agree to the practices described below.

1. What data we collect

Darija Translator collects the following data only when you choose to log in and contribute translations:

  • Email address — collected via Google Sign-In and used solely to attribute your contributions to your account.
  • Display name — collected via Google Sign-In for the same purpose.
  • Translations you contribute — any English-to-Darija translations you submit are stored in our database to improve the shared dataset.

If you use the extension without logging in, no personal data is collected. Translation lookups are performed locally using a bundled dictionary and no data is sent to any server.

2. How we use your data

We use the data we collect exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To attribute crowdsourced translations to the correct contributor
  • To maintain the quality and integrity of the translation dataset
  • To allow administrators to review and moderate contributions

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, analytics, or any purpose unrelated to the extension's translation functionality.

3. How we store your data

Your data is stored securely in Google Firestore, a cloud database provided by Google. Your login state is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API and is never transmitted to any third party.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. Your email address is visible only to the extension administrator for moderation purposes and is never displayed publicly.

The only third-party service we use is Google Firebase / Firestore for database storage and authentication, which is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data retention

Your data is retained for as long as you have an active account or until you request deletion. To request deletion of your data, please contact us at the email below and we will remove your contributions and account information within 30 days.

6. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data at any time
  • Withdraw your consent by simply not logging in or uninstalling the extension

7. Children's privacy

Darija Translator is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
